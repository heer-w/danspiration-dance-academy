Our Story

About Dancepiration

Dancepiration Dance Academy & Fitness Center is Indore's vibrant hub where dance, fitness, and community come together like a family.

Located in the heart of Indore at Siddharth Nagar, Kesar Bagh Road, we offer a warm, energetic environment for all age groups — from tiny tots to adults — to discover their passion for dance and build a healthier lifestyle.

We celebrate every festival together — from Janmashtami and Republic Day to Christmas and New Year — because we believe that a family that dances together, grows together.
